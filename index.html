Frequently Asked Questions

Does the referral cost me anything?

No. The referral fee is paid between agents from the commission — you never pay extra for the connection.

What if I'm moving to a state where you don't have contacts?

Through HomeSmart's national network and platforms I work with, I can source agents in all 50 states. If I don't have a personal connection, I'll do the legwork to find someone who's the right fit for you.

How far in advance should I reach out?

The earlier the better — ideally 3 to 6 months before your move. That said, I've helped clients coordinate last-minute relocations too. Reach out whenever you are.

Can you help with rentals too?

Yes, I can connect you with agents who specialize in rental placement in your destination market, particularly useful for buyers who need a temporary landing spot.

What areas of California do you cover?

I'm licensed throughout California and focus primarily on Orange County and Los Angeles County
